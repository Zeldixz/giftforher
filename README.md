## üíù The Story

Jose and Gabby were enjoying a date at Freddy's Pizzeria when disaster struck. The animatronics suddenly activated, and Golden Freddy dragged Gabby away to the main stage. Now Jose must brave the haunted pizzeria, fighting through hordes of animatronics and defeating powerful bosses to save the love of his life.

Throughout the journey, Jose finds clues about Gabby's location - her bracelet, security logs, audio recordings, and video footage. Each discovery strengthens his resolve. No obstacle is too great, no enemy too fearsome. Love will guide him through the darkness.
